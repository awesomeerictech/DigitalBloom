# Plugins & Extensions

DigitalBloom uses a **plugin-based architecture** to extend functionality without changing the core platform.

---

## What Plugins Can Do

- Add custom marketing layouts
- Enable analytics and reporting
- Integrate external services
- Provide AI-assisted features (optional)
- Support industry-specific workflows

---

## Plugin Availability

- Some plugins are included in Community Edition
- Advanced or specialised plugins may be available through commercial engagement

---

## Developing Plugins

Plugin development guidelines and SDKs may be provided separately under commercial or partner agreements.

Contact Stratetactical for details.
