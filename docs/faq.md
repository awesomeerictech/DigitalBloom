# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is DigitalBloom an ERP?
No. DigitalBloom is a marketing and communication platform. It does not manage accounting, inventory, or internal business operations.

## Do customers need to install an app?
No. Customers access content via QR codes using their browser.

## Is the platform offline-capable?
Yes. DigitalBloom works in offline and low-connectivity environments.

## Is the source code open?
The Community Edition distributes binaries only. Source access may be available under commercial terms.

## Can I white-label DigitalBloom?
Yes, under commercial agreements.
