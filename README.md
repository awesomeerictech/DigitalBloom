## DigitalBloom

**DigitalBloom** is a **cross-platform marketing and communication platform** for anyone who sells products or services.
It enables businesses to attract customers, present value clearly, and sustain growth through **QR-driven digital experiences** that work online, offline, and at the edge.

DigitalBloom is **not an ERP**. It does not manage accounting, inventory, or internal enterprise workflows.
Its sole focus is **customer-facing marketing, communication, and engagement**.

---

## What DigitalBloom Does

* Generates **marketing links and QR codes** that connect physical touchpoints to digital content
* Runs **marketing pages locally, on embedded devices, or in the cloud**
* Supports **rich media** (images, video, live streams)
* Enables **fast content updates** using metadata-driven workflows
* Operates reliably in **low-connectivity and offline-first environments**
* Extends functionality through a **plugin-based architecture**

## Platform, Not an App

DigitalBloom is a **platform**, not a single mobile application.

It runs consistently on:

* **Android**
* **Windows**
* **Linux**
* **macOS**
* **Embedded systems** (e.g. Raspberry Pi)
* **Cloud VPS environments**

Deployment options include:

* On-premise inside a business
* Local Wi-Fi or hotspot access for walk-in customers
* Public cloud hosting
* Hybrid edge + cloud configurations

Businesses choose **where and how** their marketing experience is delivered.

---

## Modular & Plugin-Based

DigitalBloom uses a **modular plugin architecture** that allows features to be added without disrupting the core platform.

Plugins can provide:

* Custom marketing layouts
* Media delivery and streaming
* Analytics and reporting
* AI-assisted insights (optional)
* Integrations with external systems

This keeps the platform lightweight, adaptable, and future-proof.

4. Generate your marketing link or QR code
5. Share the QR code with customers

No app installation is required for end users.
